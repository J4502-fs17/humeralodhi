Columbia will always be home to me-- and Mizzou has always played a big role in making Columbia feel like a home. I know I’ll be coming back to watch football game, to bring my children to watch the homecoming parade and visit the house decs. As a student adjusting to a college schedule, my tutors helped me get an A in my classes. The various activities-- like Mizzou After Dark-- all helped create a sense of community. Through organizations like the Tiger Pantry, I saw the importance Mizzou places on service. There have been so many people at Mizzou who have reached out to help me and make me feel like part of a community. I want to give back to those people, those causes, and those organizations. I hope to provide aid in any way needed. I want to keep up with the organizations I am involved in and dedicate my time and skills in the future to help students in the way the people of MU helped me in my education and college experience.
